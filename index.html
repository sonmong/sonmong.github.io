Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?

Our garden center is open Mon-Tue-Thur-Fri-Sat 9am-6pm, Sunday 11am-4pm, and closed on Wednesdays and holidays. Our landscape and nursery services are available by appointment only.

Do you offer landscaping consultations?

Yes, we offer professional landscaping consultations for both residential and commercial properties. Please call us at 513-523-2427 to schedule an appointment.

What types of plants do you have at your garden center?

We carry a wide variety of trees, shrubs, perennials, annuals, ground covers, herbs, and vegetables. We also offer seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, herbicides, and pest deterrents.

Do you offer delivery for your products?

Yes, we provide delivery services for our products, including mulch, topsoil, compost, gravel, sand, stone, pavers, and boulders, within the Oxford area.

Our Farm

We Love Our Farm!

In 1894 our Great-Great Uncle Rolla Drake and his wife Orie built up the farm where Shademakers was started in 1991. Although our Garden Center has moved into town, the farm is still home to our landscape operations, greenhouses and production nursery. Over the years some things have changed but most have stayed the same. In the big barn, we still have many of the contraptions that were used to work the land generations past. The land around the Nursery is still farmed today, but by modern methods.

The nursery is still open to the public for a few items not available in town, but by appointment only.
